Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our Instagram Hashtag Tracker collects and processes the following types of information:

  • API Configuration Data: Instagram API credentials (Client ID, Client Secret, Access Tokens)
  • Search Data: Hashtags and search queries you enter
  • Instagram Content Data: Public Instagram posts, including captions, media URLs, engagement metrics
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with our application (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To retrieve and display Instagram content based on your hashtag searches
  • To provide analytics and insights on Instagram engagement
  • To improve our service functionality and user experience
  • To ensure compliance with Instagram's API terms of service

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: API credentials are stored locally in your browser only
  • No Server Storage: We do not store your API credentials on our servers
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Data Retention: Search results are cached temporarily for performance

4. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Instagram API: To fetch public content and user data
  • Facebook Graph API: For authentication and data access

These services have their own privacy policies that govern the use of your data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request export of your data
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing
